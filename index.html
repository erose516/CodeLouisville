About Handy Miss

As a pre-teen girl before the days of cell phones, a young Handy Miss wanted a phone in her room. "But you don't have a telephone jack," her mom pointed out. "If I install a phone jack," asked Handy Miss, "can I have a phone then?" Mom smiled. "Sure," she replied. After raiding the toolbox, taking apart a phone jack in another room and raiding it for parts, Handy Miss had a working phone jack and became a do-it-yourself-er!
